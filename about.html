Dalton Warehouse

Dalton Warehouse is a collaborative studio and exhibition space located in South Central LA, and run by artists:

  • Katie Kirk
  • Aubrey Ingmar Manson
  • Lydia Pfeffer
  • Daniel Schubert
  • Keith Tolch

Through a democratic system of curatorial projects developed by a rotating cast of codirectors, Dalton Warehouse engages with and expands a growing art community open to thought and provocation.
